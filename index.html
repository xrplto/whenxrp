How much XRP is left on exchanges?
Exchange reserves fluctuate daily. Generally, when XRP leaves exchanges, it signals long-term holding sentiment (bullish). When XRP flows to exchanges, it may indicate selling pressure. Track on-chain data at xrpscan.com for real-time reserve metrics.
How much XRP does Ripple own?
Ripple holds a significant portion of the total XRP supply, with most locked in escrow. Each month, 1 billion XRP is released from escrow, with unused portions returned. This creates predictable supply. Check Ripple's quarterly reports for exact figures.
